Cat

Cats have been associated with humans for at least 9,500 years.

  • Trinomial name: Felis silvestris catus
  • Conservation status: Domesticated
  • Weigh: From 5.5 to 16 pounds
  • Average time of sleeping: 2/3 of every day

Domestic cats, especially young kittens, are known for their love of play. Cats communicate by meowing, purring, hissing, growling, squeaking, chiring, clicking, and grunting. They are known for their cleanliness and grooming themselves by licking their fur.
